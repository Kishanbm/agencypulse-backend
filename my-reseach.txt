1. Overview
A SaaS platform for marketing agencies to collect, analyze, visualize, and report marketing data from multiple platforms.
 
2. Roles
- Platform Owner (Admin)
- Agency (Customer)
- Client (Agency’s client)
 
3. Core Workflow
1. Add Client
2. Connect Platforms (OAuth)
3. Fetch Data (APIs)
4. Store Data (Time-series DB)
5. Dashboard Display
6. KPI Calculations
7. Report Generation
8. Alerts & Notifications
9. Client Portal
10. White Labeling

A complete SaaS platform that:

- Offers automation and AI insights
